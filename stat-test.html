Corrected power formulas now stored on page

Character stats are the black card values, before equipment. Equipped green stats = character stats + weapon/accessory stats. CharacterBasePower = CharacterHP × 12 + CharacterATK × 60 MasteryPower ≈ CharacterBasePower × 0.0025 × MasteryLevel EquipmentPower = WeaponHP × 6 + WeaponATK × 30 + AccessoryHP × 6 + AccessoryATK × 30 + AccessorySPD × 180 UnitPower = CharacterBasePower + MasteryPower + EquipmentPower Important correction from Venus B148: Do not feed green equipped stats into CharacterBasePower. Do not add EquipmentPower after already adding equipment into character stats. Do not use 0.005 mastery coefficient; that overinflates power. Stat reconstruction layer is intentionally left alone on this test page.
